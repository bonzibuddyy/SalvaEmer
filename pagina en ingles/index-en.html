Introduction

Salva-Emer is an organization based in the Canary Islands, specializing in emergency and rescue services, operating throughout Spain. Salva-Emer was born from the vocation and commitment of a group of volunteers with extensive experience in emergency and rescue services, united to improve the safety and well-being of people when they need it most. Over the years, Salva-Emer has grown and now has a multidisciplinary team of highly trained professionals, ready to act quickly and efficiently in critical situations. Our priority is the safety and well-being of people, offering innovative solutions through the use of advanced technology and continuous training. Serving the well-being of people, transparency, and collaboration are the pillars that guide us in every mission.
